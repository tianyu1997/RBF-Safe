# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, welcome
different levels of experience, give actionable technical feedback, and
discuss disagreements with evidence. Examples of unacceptable behavior
include harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate disruption,
publishing another person's private information, and retaliation against a
good-faith reporter.

## Safety discussions

Safety and certification claims should receive especially careful review.
Questioning a claim or requesting evidence is welcome; overstating certainty,
hiding known limitations, or pressuring someone to suppress a correctness
concern is not.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, issues, or other
participation that violates this standard, and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation. Reports should be sent privately through the channel
in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when they contain sensitive information.

Maintainers will avoid conflicts of interest where practical, keep reports
confidential to the extent possible, and explain enforcement decisions to the
affected participant.
